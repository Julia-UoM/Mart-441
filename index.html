About Me

I am a student interested in game design, storytelling, and interactive media. I enjoy exploring how games can create emotional experiences and bring people together.

  • Major: Game Design
  • Expected Graduation: 2026
  • Interests: Art, coding, worldbuilding

A Game Memory

One of my strongest gaming memories is playing Minecraft with friends late at night. We built massive structures, explored caves, and slowly created a shared world. It felt less like a game and more like a digital place we lived in together.

How I See the World

I see the world as a system of interconnected stories. Games reflect that. They let us experiment with choices, consequences, and identities in a safe space. I think games can help us understand complex problems by letting us play with them.
